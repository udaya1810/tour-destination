Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

Day 1

Day 1: Pick up and Arrival Srinagar

Welcome !! Upon your arrival at Srinagar Airport, our representative will warmly greet you and assist in transferring you to a Deluxe Houseboat. Spend the afternoon at leisure, engaging in personal activities, and relishing the picturesque setting. Overnight stay at the Houseboat.

End of Day

Day 2

Day 2: Srinagar – Local Sightseeing (Approx. 50 Kms)

Embark on a half-day excursion to the renowned Mughal Gardens – Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Bagh. Experience the delightful essence of these gardens, symbols of Mughal opulence and grandeur. Following a hot lunch (optional), indulge in a tranquil Shikara ride on the mesmerizing Dal Lake, exploring its captivating surroundings. Overnight stay at the Houseboat.

End of Day

Day 3

Day 3: Srinagar to Gulmarg (Approx. 56 Kms)

Post breakfast, venture towards Gulmarg, a captivating summer resort renowned for its scenic beauty. At an elevation of 8700 ft, revel in the panoramic views and indulge in various activities. Witness the highest golf course globally, relishing the splendor of grassy slopes adorned with wildflowers. Optionally, take the exhilarating Gondola Cable Car ride to Kongdori and Apharwat range, offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas. Overnight stay in Gulmarg.

End of Day

Day 4

Day 4: Gulmarg to Pahalgam (Approx. 149 Kms)

After breakfast, depart from Gulmarg to Pahalgam, nestled at 2440 meters in the Vale of Kashmir. En route, explore the saffron fields and Avantipur ruins, housing an ancient temple dating back eleven centuries. Reach Pahalgam by lunchtime, known as the Indian Film Industry’s favorite location. Spend the rest of the day embracing nature’s charm. Overnight stay at a hotel in Pahalgam.

End of Day

Day 5

Day 5: Pahalgam to Srinagar – Departure

Bid adieu to the enchanting beauty of Pahalgam and head back to Srinagar. Our team will ensure your timely transfer to the airport for your onward flight, concluding this mesmerizing 5-day journey through Kashmir’s paradisiacal landscapes.

End of Day
